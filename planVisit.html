Visitor Information

Hours: 9.30 AM – 4.00 PM

Tickets: Available online and at the gate

Location: Woodland Park, Seattle

Getting Here

By Car: Ample parking available in the North and South lots.

By Bus: Metro routes 5, 44, and E Line stop near the zoo.

By Bike: Bike racks available at both entrances.

Parking Information

  • Parking Fee: $6 for the first 2 hours, $2 per additional hour.
  • EV Charging Stations available near West Entrance.
  • Accessible parking spaces provided.

Accessibility

The zoo is wheelchair-friendly with accessible routes throughout.

  • Wheelchair rentals available at the main entrance
  • Accessible restrooms at key exhibit areas
  • Service animals permitted

Dining Options

Enjoy a variety of food choices during your visit:

  • Rain Forest Food Pavilion: Burgers, fries, drinks
  • Jungle Hut Café: Snacks, ice creams, juices
  • Pizza Corner: Family-friendly meal combos

Special Events

  • Animal Feeding Sessions – Daily at 11 AM & 2 PM
  • Bird Show – Weekends at 1 PM
  • Night Zoo Experience – Summer season only

Tips for Your Visit

  • Arrive early to avoid crowds
  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking
  • Carry a water bottle—refill stations available
  • Check weather and bring umbrellas if needed
